How It Works
Most ebook devices and apps give you a personal "send-to-device" email address. When you email an ebook to that address, it is delivered to your device/library.
Many providers require approved senders, so the sender address you use here may need to be whitelisted in your device account settings.
Book sources let you choose which service and URL Easereader uses to search for and download books.
Setup flow:
1. Add a sender: your SMTP account that sends emails.
2. Add a profile: your device email + which sender should be used.
3. From search results, click a profile avatar to send the ebook directly.
Choose the active search/download strategy and one URL for each source.
